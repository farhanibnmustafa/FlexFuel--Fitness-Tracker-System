Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 1, 2026

You can request deletion of your FlexFule account and related personal data using one of the options below.

Option 1: Delete from Account Settings

Sign in to FlexFule, open Settings, then choose Delete Account. This removes your account and associated profile data from our active systems.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to farhanibnmustafa@gmail.com with subject line Data Deletion Request from your registered email address. We will verify ownership and process the request.

Processing Time

We typically process deletion requests within 7 business days. Some records may be retained temporarily where required by law or for security/audit purposes.
